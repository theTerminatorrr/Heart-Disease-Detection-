Dataset & Features

13 clinical features per patient — age, sex, chest pain type, resting blood pressure, cholesterol, fasting blood sugar, resting ECG, max heart rate, exercise-induced angina, ST depression, ST slope, vessels colored by fluoroscopy, and thalassemia — predicting a binary target: presence (1) or absence (0) of heart disease.
